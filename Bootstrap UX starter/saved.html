About T-Connect

What is the T-Connect Programme?

T-Connect is a digital T-Level pilot created by Manchester Digital. The T-Connect Programme offers T-Level students the opportunity to attend a 45-day industry placement. Industry placements are a key part of T Levels. These placements give students the opportunity to develop real skills in a real workplace.


What are T-Levels?

T-Levels are 2-year courses which are taken after GCSEs and are equivalent in size to 3 A-Levels. T-Levels have been developed in collaboration with employers and education providers so that the content meets the needs of industry and prepares students for entry into skilled employment, an apprenticeship or related technical study through further or higher education.
